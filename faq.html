Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions.

What are the club's goals and objectives for the upcoming season?


Our goals for the upcoming season include improving team chemistry, honing individual skills, and competing at a high level in our league.

How does the club support player development, both on and off the court?


We have dedicated coaches and training programs focused on skill development, fitness, and game strategy to support our players' growth.

What opportunities are available for members to engage with the local community or participate in community service projects?


We actively encourage our members to engage in community service projects and regularly organize events to give back to the local community.

How does the club promote inclusivity and diversity among its members?


Inclusivity and diversity are core values of our club, and we strive to create a welcoming environment for players of all backgrounds.

What kind of training and coaching staff does the club have, and what qualifications do they bring?


Our coaching staff includes experienced professionals with a deep understanding of the game and a passion for helping players reach their full potential.

Can you describe the club's approach to balancing academics and athletics for student-athletes?


We prioritize academic success and work closely with student-athletes to ensure they have the resources and support needed to excel in both areas.

What resources does the club provide to support student-athletes' mental and physical well-being?


We provide access to sports psychologists, nutritionists, and athletic trainers to support the mental and physical well-being of our players.

Are there opportunities for club members to compete in tournaments or leagues outside of regular season play?


Our club participates in various tournaments and leagues outside of our regular season schedule to provide additional competitive opportunities for our members.
