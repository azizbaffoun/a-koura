CLUB ACHIEVEMENTS

Undefeated has consistently etched its name in the annals of sporting history. Since its inception, our club has been a beacon of inspiration, fostering a legacy built on dedication, resilience, and unwavering determination.

Legends' Legacy Cup 2020

Set in an alternate history where legendary figures from the past are resurrected to form baseball teams, the Legends' Legacy Cup brings together iconic players from different eras to compete for glory.

The Elemental Championship 2023

In a fantasy world where magic and myth reign supreme, baseball teams harness the power of the elements to compete in the Elemental Championship.

The Stellar Series 2021

In this futuristic baseball championship, teams compete in a specially designed stadium located on a space station orbiting Earth.

Our foundation rests upon the unbreakable bond of unity. We stand not just as a team but as a family, united by a singular passion that fuels our aspirations and drives us towards greatness.

We are United.
